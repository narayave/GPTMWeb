Membership

Members are the sustaining pillars of GPTM. Apart from the great savings and incredible value that comes with being a GPTM member, what is most valuable is your continual support and involvement with a community that connects Tamil families and our culture. Membership helps greatly with planning and execution of high quality events. Membership also helps gain support of generous sponsors.
